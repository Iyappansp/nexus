2. Slip Rental Agreement

2.1 Rental Period

Boat slip rentals are available on a daily, monthly, or annual basis. The rental period begins on the date specified in your rental agreement and continues until the end date or until properly terminated.

2.2 Payment Terms

  • Payment is due in advance for the rental period
  • Late payments may incur additional fees
  • Security deposits are required for all rentals
  • Refunds are subject to our cancellation policy

2.3 Cancellation Policy

  • Daily rentals: 24-hour notice required for full refund
  • Monthly rentals: 7-day notice required
  • Annual rentals: 30-day notice required

3. Vessel Requirements

3.1 Documentation

All vessels must have:

  • Valid registration and documentation
  • Current insurance coverage
  • Proper safety equipment

3.2 Vessel Condition

Vessels must be seaworthy and in good operating condition. The marina reserves the right to refuse or remove any vessel that poses a safety hazard or does not meet our standards.

4. Marina Rules and Regulations

4.1 Operating Hours

The marina is accessible 24/7 to slip renters. Office hours are Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Saturday-Sunday 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM.

4.2 Safety Requirements

  • No swimming in the marina
  • Speed limit: 5 mph within marina waters
  • Proper fender and line usage required
  • No live-aboard without prior approval
  • Pets must be leashed at all times

4.3 Prohibited Activities

  • Major vessel repairs or maintenance
  • Discharge of waste or pollutants
  • Excessive noise after 10:00 PM
  • Commercial activities without permission

5. Liability and Insurance

5.1 Renter's Responsibility

Renters are responsible for:

  • All damage to their vessel
  • Damage to marina property caused by their vessel
  • Injuries to third parties caused by their vessel or actions

5.2 Marina Liability

Harbor Marina is not responsible for:

  • Damage to vessels from weather, acts of nature, or other vessels
  • Theft or vandalism of vessels or personal property
  • Loss or damage due to fire, storm, or other casualties

5.3 Insurance Requirements

All renters must maintain comprehensive marine insurance with minimum liability coverage of $300,000. Proof of insurance must be provided before occupying a slip.

6. Maintenance and Utilities

6.1 Marina Responsibilities

6.2 Renter Responsibilities

  • Reporting maintenance issues promptly

7. Termination

The marina reserves the right to terminate any rental agreement for:
