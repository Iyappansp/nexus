Find Us

Location & Access

Our marina is conveniently located with clear entry routes and easy access to nearby coastal destinations. Navigation into the marina is simple and suitable for both experienced and new boaters.

Getting Here

By Car: Take Highway 1 to Harbor Drive exit. Follow signs to the marina entrance. Ample parking available for guests and slip holders.

By Water: Navigate to coordinates 34.0522° N, 118.2437° W. Look for our clearly marked entrance buoys and follow the channel markers into the protected harbor.
