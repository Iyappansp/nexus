Flexible Options

Boat Slip Rentals

We offer flexible boat slip rental options to suit different needs and vessel sizes. Our slips accommodate fishing boats, sailboats, speedboats, and yachts.

Small Vessels

Slips for boats up to 25 feet, perfect for fishing boats, small sailboats, and personal watercraft.

  • 20-25 ft slips available
  • Power and water hookups
  • Easy dock access

Medium Vessels

Accommodations for boats 26-40 feet, ideal for cruisers, sailboats, and larger fishing vessels.

  • 26-40 ft slips available
  • 30/50 amp electrical service
  • Floating dock options

Large Vessels & Yachts

Premium slips for vessels over 40 feet, including yachts and large cruisers with full amenities.

  • 40+ ft slips available
  • 100 amp service available
  • Premium dock locations

Slip Facilities

Every slip comes equipped with essential utilities and features for your convenience.

Multiple Slip Sizes Available

Various slip sizes to accommodate vessels from small fishing boats to large yachts, ensuring the perfect fit for your boat.

Fresh Water Connections

Convenient fresh water hookups at every slip for easy access to clean water whenever you need it.

Electrical Power Supply

Reliable electrical connections with various amperage options (30/50/100 amp) to keep your vessel powered.

Secure Mooring Points

Professional-grade cleats and mooring equipment designed for safe and secure docking in all conditions.

Well-Lit Docks for Night Access

LED lighting throughout the marina ensures safe navigation and access to your vessel at any hour.
